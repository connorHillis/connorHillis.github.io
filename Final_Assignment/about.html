About

This website contains four web pages. Explanations of each and navigation to each of them are provided below. The intention of this website is to provide prospective vacationers ideas for destinations and activities offered by California.

Home Page

The home page displays our 8 most popular attractions in California. The description above each video may be clicked in order to bring up a short video about that attraction in a pop-up window. The Popular Attractions button on the jumbotron takes you to the bottom of the webpage where the pictures and videos of these attractions may be seen. The other three webpages may be accesses using the top navigation bar.

Top 5's Page

The Top 5's page is the page where a user may explore all of the activities and destinations by category. They may also filter these top 5's to see which are located in the counties that they plan to visit. These categories include the following:


  • Active - Primarily includes hiking trails.
  • Relaxing - Primarily includes resorts and spas.
  • Scenic - Primarily includes National Parks and beaches.
  • Thrill Seeking - Includes destinations for activities such as paragliding, zip lining, and theme parks that fit this category.
  • Dining/Restaurants - Includes the best restaurants.
  • Lodging/Hotels - Includes the best hotels.

Note: The above destinations are the best options in the low-to-mid price range.

Contact Page

The Form page contains input elements for the website visitor's name, contact information, and a text area where they may input any questions that they would like to submit to the California Vacation Services' travel agents. The user must see the dialog box saying "Your form has been submitted successfully!" upon clicking the Submit button in order to be assured that their form has been submitted properly.
